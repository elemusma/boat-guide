About the Handbook of Sailing

The aim of this guide is to teach a complex and technical sport in an easi to understand manner for everyone to understand. Recently, sailing has evolved in incredible ways. There are a lot of different varieties of sailing - from inshore and offshore sailing, racing and world-wide voyages. The author, Bob Bond, has created training programs that are now used worldwide, which helped him expand his knowledge in handling modern boats and teaching the art of sailing. The techniques are shown in both diagrams and photos using a step-by-step system. All illustrations have a thorough explanation to help the reader understand better.
